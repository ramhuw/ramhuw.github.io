About

My name is Hechi Zhang (張 和持), born in 1998 in Leshan (樂山), currently a graduate student in the department of mathematics at Indiana University Bloomington since 2024, under Prof. Matthias Strauch. I have a dream to pursue further in number theory and physics, and this site serves as my blog.

From 2022 to 2024, I studied at Leiden University (Universiteit Leiden) and earned my master of science degree under Eugenia Rosu, with thesis Hilbert Modular Surfaces and Hirzebruch-Zagier Cycles.

From 2017 to 2021, I studied at Beijing University of Chemical Technology (北京化工大學) and earned my bachelar of science degree.
