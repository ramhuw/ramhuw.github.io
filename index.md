## About

My name is [Hechi Zhang](https://math.indiana.edu/about/graduate-students/zhang-hechi.html) (張 和持), born in 1998 in Leshan (樂山), China and currently a graduate student in the Department of Mathematics at Indiana University Bloomington since 2024, under [Prof. Matthias Strauch](https://math.indiana.edu/about/faculty/strauch-matthias.html). I have a dream to pursue further in number theory and physics, and this site serves as my blog.

From 2022 to 2024, I studied at Leiden University (Universiteit Leiden) and earned my master of science degree under [Eugenia Rosu](https://sites.google.com/view/eugeniarosu/home), with thesis [Hilbert Modular Surfaces and Hirzebruch-Zagier Cycles](https://studenttheses.universiteitleiden.nl/handle/1887/4104713).

From 2017 to 2021, I studied at Beijing University of Chemical Technology (北京化工大學) and earned my bachelar of science degree.
